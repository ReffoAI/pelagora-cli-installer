## Email 3: How it actually works
**Subject:** No jargon, no catch — how Reffo works
**Send:** Day 4

People ask how Reffo is different. Here's the short version:

**Traditional marketplace:**
You → list on their site → they decide who sees it → they take a cut → they keep your data

**Reffo:**
You → tell Reffo Bot what you want to sell → it goes live on an open network → buyers find you directly → you keep everything

The "open network" part is Pelagora — an open-source protocol for peer-to-peer commerce. It's like email: anyone can run a server, anyone can trade with anyone, and no single company controls it.

What that gets you:

- **List in under a minute.** Chat, scan a photo, or scan a barcode.
- **Zero listing fees.** What you sell for is what you keep.
- **Your data is yours.** Export it, move it, delete it.
- **Reverse auctions.** Post what you want to buy. Sellers compete on price.
- **Peerseller embeds.** Share your listing anywhere. Anyone who helps sell it earns a commission.

No blockchain. No tokens. Nothing weird.
